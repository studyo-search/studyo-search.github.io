Studyo

Search studios the way you've always wanted.
Studyo is a work-in-progress studio finder and searcher. It will allow you to search for certain studios with certain descriptions, curators, or comments, and search for comments inside of studios. Studyo is also meant to be working with user comments too, but that may be delayed for a bit. Development will be headed on Replit. Anybody who wants to contribute can post on the Advanced Topics thread on Scratch with their Replit username and some examples of their work.
